## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Installer is engaged in the business of designing, installing, and servicing smart home technology systems for residential properties;

WHEREAS, Builder is engaged in the business of constructing residential homes in the Wichita, Kansas metropolitan area;

WHEREAS, Builder desires to offer smart home technology packages to its home buyers as an optional upgrade, and Installer desires to provide such installation services;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set forth and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Installation Services"** means the design, procurement, installation, configuration, testing, and homeowner orientation of Smart Home Packages in Builder's residential properties.

**1.2 "Smart Home Package"** means one of the standardized smart home technology bundles offered by Installer, as described in Exhibit A attached hereto.

**1.3 "Home"** means a single-family residential dwelling unit constructed by Builder within the Territory.

**1.4 "Territory"** means the Wichita, Kansas metropolitan statistical area, including Sedgwick County and immediately adjacent counties.

**1.5 "Homeowner"** means the purchaser of a Home from Builder who has elected to include a Smart Home Package.

**1.6 "Construction Schedule"** means Builder's timeline for construction phases of each Home, including rough-in and trim-out dates.

---

## ARTICLE 2: SCOPE OF SERVICES

**2.1 Installation Services.** Installer agrees to provide Installation Services for Smart Home Packages selected by Homeowners in connection with the purchase of Homes from Builder.

**2.2 Package Options.** Installer shall offer the Smart Home Packages described in Exhibit A, which may be updated by mutual written agreement of the Parties.

**2.3 Standards.** All Installation Services shall be performed:
- (a) In accordance with applicable building codes and regulations;
- (b) Using the equipment and materials specified in Exhibit A;
- (c) By trained and qualified technicians;
- (d) In coordination with Builder's Construction Schedule.

**2.4 Scope Limitations.** This Agreement covers Installation Services for new construction Homes only. Post-construction modifications, upgrades, or retrofits for existing homeowners are not covered under this Agreement and may be provided by Installer under separate terms.

---

## ARTICLE 3: BUILDER OBLIGATIONS

**3.1 Sales Inclusion.** Builder agrees to:
- (a) Include Installer's Smart Home Packages as options in Builder's standard home purchase agreement and options sheet;
- (b) Present Smart Home Package options to prospective Homeowners;
- (c) Ensure Builder's sales representatives attend Installer-provided training.

**3.2 Construction Coordination.** Builder agrees to:
- (a) Provide Installer with a minimum of fourteen (14) days' advance notice prior to rough-in phase of each Home requiring Installation Services;
- (b) Provide Installer with reasonable access to Homes during appropriate construction phases;
- (c) Coordinate Installation Services with electrical, HVAC, and other relevant trades;
- (d) Notify Installer promptly of any Construction Schedule changes affecting Installation Services.

**3.3 Information Sharing.** Builder agrees to provide Installer with:
- (a) Floor plans and electrical layouts for each Home model;
- (b) Homeowner contact information for completed installations;
- (c) Projected construction volumes on a quarterly basis.

---

## ARTICLE 4: INSTALLER OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Training.** Installer agrees to provide, at no cost to Builder:
- (a) Initial sales training for Builder's sales representatives (approximately five hours);
- (b) Ongoing training updates as needed;
- (c) Sales support materials including brochures, comparison sheets, and digital assets.

**4.2 Model Home Program.** Upon request, Installer agrees to install a Smart Home Package in one (1) of Builder's model homes at Installer's cost (equipment and labor), subject to the terms in Exhibit B.

**4.3 Timeline Compliance.** Installer agrees to:
- (a) Complete rough-in work within two (2) business days of scheduled start date;
- (b) Complete trim-out and final installation within three (3) business days of scheduled start date;
- (c) Conduct homeowner orientation prior to closing.

**4.4 Quality Standards.** Installer agrees to:
- (a) Perform all work in a professional and workmanlike manner;
- (b) Leave work areas clean and free of debris;
- (c) Complete a punch-list walkthrough with Builder prior to homeowner orientation;
- (d) Address any punch-list items within two (2) business days.

**4.5 Insurance.** Installer shall maintain throughout the term of this Agreement:
- (a) Commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $1,000,000 per occurrence;
- (b) Workers' compensation insurance as required by Kansas law;
- (c) Commercial auto liability insurance with limits of not less than $500,000 per occurrence.

Upon request, Installer shall provide Builder with certificates of insurance naming Builder as an additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 5: PRICING AND PAYMENT

**5.1 Package Pricing.** Builder shall pay Installer the prices set forth in Exhibit A for each Smart Home Package installed. Prices are firm for twelve (12) months from the Effective Date and may be adjusted annually upon thirty (30) days' written notice.

**5.2 Payment Terms.** Installer shall invoice Builder upon completion of each installation. Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date. Late payments shall bear interest at the rate of 1.5% per month.

**5.3 Builder Pricing.** Builder may set its own retail pricing for Smart Home Packages to Homeowners. Installer makes no representations regarding minimum or maximum retail pricing.

**5.4 Taxes.** All prices are exclusive of applicable sales and use taxes, which shall be the responsibility of Builder to collect from Homeowners and remit to appropriate authorities.

---

## ARTICLE 6: WARRANTY AND SUPPORT

**6.1 Product Warranty.** Smart home equipment installed under this Agreement shall carry manufacturers' warranties as specified in Exhibit A. Installer shall assist Homeowners in processing warranty claims with manufacturers.

**6.2 Workmanship Warranty.** Installer warrants that all Installation Services shall be free from defects in workmanship for a period of two (2) years from the date of installation completion.

**6.3 Warranty Service.** During the warranty period, Installer shall:
- (a) Respond to warranty service requests within one (1) business day;
- (b) Complete warranty repairs within three (3) business days of diagnosis;
- (c) Provide all warranty service at no cost to Builder or Homeowner.

**6.4 Builder Indemnification.** Installer shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Builder from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from:
- (a) Defects in Installer's workmanship;
- (b) Damage to Builder's property caused by Installer;
- (c) Personal injury to Installer's employees occurring on Builder's job sites.

**6.5 Limitations.** Installer's warranty does not cover:
- (a) Damage caused by misuse, neglect, or unauthorized modification;
- (b) Damage caused by power surges, lightning, or acts of God;
- (c) Normal wear and tear of equipment;
- (d) Issues arising from Builder's or Homeowner's Wi-Fi or internet service.

## ARTICLE 7: TERM AND TERMINATION

**7.1 Initial Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of one (1) year ("Initial Term").

**7.2 Renewal.** Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods ("Renewal Terms") unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

**7.3 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the other Party:
- (a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within the notice period;
- (b) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy protection;
- (c) Ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course.

**7.4 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon ninety (90) days' written notice.

**7.5 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination:
- (a) Installer shall complete all Installation Services for Homes under contract as of the termination date;
- (b) Builder shall pay Installer for all completed installations;
- (c) Installer shall continue to provide warranty service for installations completed prior to termination;
- (d) Each Party shall return or destroy confidential information of the other Party.

---

**8.1 Confidential Information.** Each Party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's confidential business information, including pricing, customer lists, and business strategies.

**8.2 Permitted Disclosures.** Confidential information may be disclosed:

**10.1 Relationship of Parties.** The Parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, employment, or agency relationship.

**10.2 Non-Exclusivity.** This Agreement is non-exclusive. Installer may provide services to other builders, and Builder may engage other smart home installers.

**10.3 Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party, except to an affiliate or in connection with a merger or acquisition.

**10.4 Force Majeure.** Neither Party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from causes beyond its reasonable control, including acts of God, natural disasters, labor disputes, or government actions.

**10.5 Notices.** All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by certified mail, overnight courier, or email to the addresses set forth above.

**10.6 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Kansas without regard to conflicts of law principles.

**10.7 Dispute Resolution.** Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through good faith negotiation. If negotiation fails, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration in Wichita, Kansas under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

**10.8 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, including all Exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.

**10.9 Amendments.** This Agreement may only be amended by written instrument signed by both Parties.

**10.10 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**10.11 Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.

## EXHIBIT B: MODEL HOME PROGRAM

### Terms and Conditions

**1. Model Home Installation**

Upon Builder's request, Installer shall install one (1) Premium Smart Home Package in a Builder-designated model home at Installer's cost, subject to the following conditions:

- (a) Builder shall identify model home location and provide access within thirty (30) days of request;
- (b) Installer shall complete installation within three (3) weeks of access;
- (c) Installation remains property of Builder upon completion.

**2. Demonstration Support**

Installer shall provide:
- (a) Professional demonstration script for Builder's sales team;
- (b) Printed and digital marketing collateral;
- (c) Quarterly technology refresh to update demonstrations;
- (d) Co-branded signage for model home display.

**3. Duration**

Model home support continues for the duration of the Agreement or until the model home is sold, whichever occurs first.

**4. Subsequent Model Homes**

Additional model home installations may be requested at Installer's then-current Builder pricing.

## EXHIBIT C: SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

### Performance Standards

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Rough-in completion | Within 2 business days of scheduled start | Per installation |
| Trim-out completion | Within 3 business days of scheduled start | Per installation |
| On-time completion rate | 95% or higher | Quarterly |
| Punch-list items at handoff | Zero | Per installation |
| Homeowner satisfaction | 4.5+ stars (5-point scale) | Quarterly survey |
| Warranty response time | Within 1 business day | Per request |
| Warranty resolution time | Within 3 business days | Per request |

### Remedies

If Installer fails to meet performance standards:

- **On-time completion below 90%**: $100 credit per late installation
- **Repeated punch-list issues**: Process review meeting within 10 days
- **Satisfaction below 4.0**: Corrective action plan within 30 days

### Reporting

Installer shall provide Builder with monthly performance reports including:
- Number of installations completed
- On-time completion rate
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Warranty claim summary
